Action « المولد النبوي الشريف »

The "المولد النبوي" action, dedicated to celebrating the birth of Prophet Muhammad, carries a unique spirit of devotion and compassion. Each year, this tradition is honored through the distribution of عصيدة زڨوڨو, a traditional and comforting dish. The first volume of this initiative was carried out in hospitals, where patients, often isolated and ill, received this dish as a small but meaningful gesture of care. The second volume reached the impoverished neighborhoods, where the less fortunate were offered this nourishing and generous meal. The process of this action unfolds in steps: first, the sponsorship outings to gather the necessary funds for ingredients, followed by the careful preparation of the عصيدة, and finally, the distribution itself, which transcends the simple act of giving food to become a shared spiritual moment. This initiative embodies the light of Islam, where every bite shared becomes a prayer, a moment of peace, and unity.
