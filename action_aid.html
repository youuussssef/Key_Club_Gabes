Eid Action

Action « عيد الفطر » Since the 2023-2024 mandate, the "عيد الفطر " initiative has emerged as a beacon of solidarity and generosity. Under the inspiring slogan "من خيركم يزهى عيد غيركم", this action aims to bring joy and smiles to underprivileged children during the holiday. The operation unfolds in three well-planned stages: first, the clothing collection stands, where individuals are invited to donate gently used clothes to those in need. Next, sponsorship outings help raise the necessary funds for purchasing new, quality clothing. Finally, the heartwarming distribution takes place, where the clothes are given to the children, turning their Eid into a moment of happiness. The "عيد الفطر " action represents the beauty of sharing, where every donation becomes a step toward a more just world, a world where the celebration of Eid is truly for everyone.

Volunteers and participants came together to distribute gifts, food, and essentials to those in need, ensuring that the festive spirit reached all corners of the community. The event highlighted the essence of giving and unity that Eid represents.

The Eid Action was a heartwarming occasion that left a lasting impact, creating a strong sense of togetherness and making the holiday even more special for everyone involved.
