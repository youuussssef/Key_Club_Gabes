About Us

The Key Club Gabes Coast is an international organization led by high school students that focuses on service, character development, and leadership skills. As the first branch in Africa, it is part of Key Club International, the oldest and largest service program for high school students. Connected to the global Kiwanis family, Key Club members are dedicated to improving their communities and making a positive impact on the lives of children. With a vision of being compassionate and competent servant leaders, the club promotes core values of leadership, character building, kindness, and inclusivity, fostering a culture where members not only serve others but also grow personally and collectively.
