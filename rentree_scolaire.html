Rentree Scolaire

The back-to-school initiative has been a core activity in our club since its founding mandate. Its main goal is to collect school supplies for primary school students. This action is organized in three phases: setting up stands at supermarkets and bookstores, sorting the supplies, and distributing them. We have carried out this project in three editions. The first edition was in collaboration with the Interact Club Gabes Oasis and the Leo Club Gabes Golfe, the second with the Tuni'Act Club Gabes, and the third again with the Interact Club Gabes Oasis.

This event involves the distribution of customized school kits to students from different backgrounds. It serves as a bridge between the community and the educational institutions, highlighting the value of solidarity and teamwork.
