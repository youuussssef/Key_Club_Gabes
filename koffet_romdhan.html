Koffet Romdhan

The "Koffet Romdhan" event was organized by KCGC World during the holy month of Ramadan. The event was focused on bringing the community together, fostering a spirit of generosity, and providing support to those in need during Ramadan.

Volunteers participated in distributing food, drinks, and essentials to families and individuals who needed them, ensuring that they could enjoy a blessed Ramadan.

The action was truly a celebration of generosity and solidarity, where the community came together to share their blessings and give back to those in need.
