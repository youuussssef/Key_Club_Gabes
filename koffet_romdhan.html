Action « قفة رمضان »

Since the founding of our club , the "قفة رمضان " action has become a sacred tradition, a symbol of generosity that intensifies as the blessed month of Ramadan approaches. Under the heartfelt slogan "نلموها من خيرنا و نعاونوا بيها غيرنا", this initiative distributes food packages to families in need, providing comfort and dignity during the month of fasting. The process unfolds in three key stages: the collection phase through donation stands and sponsorship outings, which gather the necessary goods and funds for the food packages; followed by careful sorting to ensure each package is complete and suited to the families’ needs; and, finally, the much-anticipated distribution, where the grateful smiles of the recipients remind us of the power of charity and solidarity. The latest volume of this action, in collaboration with the Interact Club Gabes Oasis under the slogan "من خيركم يعمر رمضان غيركم", added an extra layer of warmth and humanity, where unity becomes strength, and hope spreads.
