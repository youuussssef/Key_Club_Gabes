Product 1

Our t-shirt isn’t just clothing; it’s a statement. On the front, it proudly displays the name of our club and the date of our founding, October 19th, 2021, a mark of our shared identity and our commitment to a cause greater than ourselves. But what sets this t-shirt apart is the design on the back. Here, you’ll discover the figure of the first person to create the key, paired with a "chachya," a small but significant detail that speaks to our Tunisian roots and cultural legacy. This design isn’t just about the past; it’s a symbol of our vision to innovate, lead, and break new ground, all while honoring where we come from. When you wear this t-shirt, you’re not just putting on fabric. You’re joining a movement. You become part of a global community committed to improving the world, شارع المفاتيح, no matter where you are.
