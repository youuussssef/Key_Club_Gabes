Tournoi FIFA

Action « Tournoi FIFA » In a different light, the "FIFA Tournament" action combines entertainment with financial goals. This tournament, a virtual battleground, brings gaming enthusiasts together for a friendly competition with a meaningful purpose: the chance to win giveaways while supporting noble causes. Each edition, whether in the 2023-2024 mandate or the 2024-2025 mandate, follows key steps: starting with sponsorship outings that fund the event, followed by stands where participants can buy tickets and prepare for the ultimate showdown. On tournament day, the atmosphere is one of camaraderie and healthy competition, uniting young people around a shared passion while generating funds for other charitable actions. The FIFA Tournament is more than just a game; it’s a means to build connections and raise funds for larger causes.

Participants engaged in intense matches, showcasing their skills and representing their teams. The tournament was a great opportunity for community members to bond over their love for football (soccer) through the FIFA game.

The event concluded with prizes and recognition for the top players, leaving everyone with a sense of achievement and camaraderie.
