Join Key Club Gabes Coast

Want to make a difference in your community? Join our vibrant club today and be part of a global movement dedicated to service and leadership.

How to join the family ?

Joining Key Club Gabes Coast is a simple, yet multistage process. The first step involves completing the standard application form, where you'll provide necessary information about yourself. This form helps us get to know you better, understand your motivations, and assess how well you align with our mission of Service and Leadership as a club. Once your application is submitted, it will be reviewed. If your application is approved, you will be invited to the next stage: recruitment. During this phase, you will participate in a short assessment and an interview. This is an opportunity for us to learn more about your values, your commitment to service, and how you plan to contribute to the club. It's also your chance to ask us questions and get a better sense of our vision. If everything goes well during this stage and you are successful, we will officially welcome you to Key Club Gabes Coast!
